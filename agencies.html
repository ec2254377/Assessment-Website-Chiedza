WHO THEY ARE



Global Environmental Agencies are organizations coming out of the conservation or environmental movements that seek to protect, analyse or monitor the environment against misuse or degradation from human forces.These organizations focus on matters that have an impact on the environment such as global warming,climate change and loss of biodiversity. The have been in partnership with GAIA for over 10 years now and have worked hard to restore nature to its form.

The Nature Conservancy


The Nature Conservancy is a gloabal envirometal non-profit working to create a world where people and nature thrive. Founded in the U.S. through grassrotts action in 1951, the Nature Conservancy (TNC) has grown to become one of the most effective and wide reaching environmental organizations in the world. Thanks to more that a million members and the dedicated efforts of our diverse staff and over 400 scientists, we impact conservation in 79 countries and territories.

GEP Enviromental


GEP Environmental are leading providers of energy and environmental consultancy services to clients across the United Kingdom. The Company formed in 2008 from the amalgamation of three companies, Energy Management Solutions, Green Energy Partners, and the Olive Consultancy. We have a diverse range of skills and services, with experts available in energy efficiency, corporate responsibility, management systems, measurement and verification, and renewable energy. We are also approved training providers for a range of environmental and waste management courses.
