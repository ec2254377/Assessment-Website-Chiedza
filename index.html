GO GREEN, LET NATURE'S BRILLIANCE BE SEEN



We’re facing two existential crises: climate change and rapid biodiversity loss. Reversing these crises requires protecting and sustainably managing our ocean, land and fresh water.The science is clear. We must radically scale up our protection efforts around the world. It’s now or never. Our best hope for a healthy and resilient planet is to protect at least 30% of lands, fresh water and ocean by 2030.


GLOBAL ASSISTANCE INTIATIVE AGENCY
An independent public body under the Environmental Protection Agency,2014. It is responsible for protecting and improving the environment as an asset value for people, companies and governments. The company’s mission is to build a future in which people live in harmony with nature reducing any immediate threat or danger to ecosystems throughout the world.
