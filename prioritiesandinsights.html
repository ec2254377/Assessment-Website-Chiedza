OUR PRIORITIES AND INSIGHTS


New Thinking For A Better Tomorrow



Lets Go Green As It Makes the Environment Clean



Our approach reflects decades of learning and refining the special role GAIA can play side by side with partners,communities and societies across the globe.


How we take action on Ocean, Land and Fresh Water




GAIA is catalyzing a full range of ocean, land and freshwater protection efforts with conservation finance, science and policy work. We are at the forefront with our partners in delivering at-scale protections and the necessary financing. We are deeply committed to supporting the ambitions and rights of communities and Indigenous Peoples and the contributions their voices, choices and actions make to conserving the diversity of life on Earth.


Some of our most innovative strategies include debt conversion, including Blue Bonds, and collaborating with other non-profits to accelerate Project Finance for Permanence (PFP) mechanisms through Enduring Earth. This work strengthens nature’s ability to save biodiversity and ourselves.
