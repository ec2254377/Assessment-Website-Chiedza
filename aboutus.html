ABOUT US


Where Our Origions Arise



Global Assistance Initiaive Agency is a non-profit organisation working towards creating a world where people and nature live in harmony



Founded in Greece throughout the grasslands action in 1951, the Global Environmental Assistance Agency has grown to become one of the most impactful environmental agencies throughout Europe. Thanks to more than a million members and the dedicated efforts of our diverse staff and vounteers, we have been able to impace 75% of the world environmental restoration.



OUR MISSION


Innovating through mission, creating solutions


To create a world where the diversity of life thrives and people act to converse nature. We also aim to protect and improve water, land, biodiversity and the way we work as a regulator to protect people and the environment and support sustainable growth

OUR VISION


Creating a New World, A New Path to the Future


To be the professional environmental agency that best understands and delivers expertise solutions to climate change and biodiversity issues. We aim to continuoisly improve our services and work to create a better world for all.


OUR HISTORY


Grounded in the science and collaborative from the beginning



Global Assistance Initiative Agency began when leading scientists,dedicated communities and general members of the public came together to make a difference to the planet after a noticed a decline in biodiversity.Today, as we take on most of the complex environmental challenges,our diverse staff have helped us impact over 50 countries world wide.

OUR JOURNEY ON THE PATH OF NATURE


1951 - 2023, Moving as a team


More than a century ago leaders gathered,debated and laid the groundwork for what would become the Global Assistance Initiative Agency.


In 1954, GAIA purchased over 3400 acres of land in Greece to set up its headquarters with hopes of restoring forests across Europe

As our planet faces the dual rapid crisis of climate change and global warming. GAIA is implementing the biggest and most ambitious plans to achieve long lasting results through science and equity.


Since 2000 GAIA has dedicated itself to improving the world we live in and restoring biodiversity across the planet.


OUR VALUES AND POLICIES


Our values define how we do business and interact with our colleagues,partners and clients



We aspire to have the highest standards of professionalism behaviour towards everyone we work with, the communities we touch and the environment on which we have an impact. You, our clienrs, are at the heart of everything we do, so we have built our values around what you can expect from us.


Understanding Our Clients' Expectations

We will take the time to work with you, our client and make it absolutely certain that we understand exactly what you want and need.


Delivering our Clients'Expectations

We strive to be the best in our field, with consultants and experts that are demonstrably capable of undertaking the work the we have contracted to deliver.


Exceeding Our Clients' Expectations

We want you to be surprised and delighted with the work that we have done for you. This means we will go the extra mile to consider alternatives that may be a value to our project.
